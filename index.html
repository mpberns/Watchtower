Protect our heroes.

In 2017, an average of 16.8 American veterans committed suicide each day. With a suicide rate that's nearly 1.5 times the national average, our heroes are in danger.

 

Watchtower is a simple Java program that mental health providers may use to assess the risk factors of their veteran patients. Combining biomarkers for PTSD alongside biomarkers and demographics associated with a higher risk of suicide, Watchtower is the most comprehesive panel to assess objective risks to our veterans.
